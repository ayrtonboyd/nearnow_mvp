NEARNOW — SMOKE TEST

Purpose:
This test ensures the LLM pipeline, dataset loading, and system prompt are functioning correctly.

How to run:
Open a browser and enter:

http://127.0.0.1:8000/api/recommend?query=Find+a+cosy+cafe+open+for+lunch+that+allows+dogs

Expected behaviour:
- Returns exactly 2–3 cafés
- All must be dog-friendly
- All must be cosy
- All must be open for lunch (10am–3pm)
- Intro line is varied, warm, and under 20 words
- Two main options under 35 words
- Third contrasting option under 25 words (if available)
- Ending line under 10 words and rotated
- No apologetic language (“only one that fits”)
- No tag lists, no bullet points
- No external knowledge or invented details
- Total ≈ 120 words or less

If any condition fails → do not deploy further code.
Fix the prompt or dataset format first.
